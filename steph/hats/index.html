Pricing

General pricing for hats

Some of these hats are currently for sale, just ask! Any hat I've already made will sell for $25. If you want a custom hat, I'm happy to do that too. I usually charge $30 - $35 for a hat, depending how much yarn and time it will use. I love using lots of different colors and materials, so feel free to be as specific as you want about what you want your hat to look like. I also don't mind being the creative element, so if you have no idea, just ask me to make you a hat!
